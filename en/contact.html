Frequently Asked Questions

How do coaching sessions work?

Sessions are conducted via video call or in person in Madrid. The first session is a discovery session (free) to get to know each other and define objectives. Subsequent sessions last 60 minutes.

In what languages do you offer sessions?

I offer sessions in Spanish, English, and French. You can choose the language you feel most comfortable with.

How long do coaching programs last?

Programs typically last between 4 and 12 sessions, depending on your goals. I also offer individual sessions without commitment.

What payment methods do you accept?

I accept bank transfer, Bizum, and credit/debit card. We issue invoices for all services.

Do you offer group sessions or for companies?

Yes, I offer group workshops and programs for companies. Contact me to customize a proposal.
